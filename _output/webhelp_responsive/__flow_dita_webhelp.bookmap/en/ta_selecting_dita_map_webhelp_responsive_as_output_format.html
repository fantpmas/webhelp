Selecting DITA Map Webhelp Responsive as output format

Proceed as follows:
  1. Click .
  2. Select DITA Map Webhelp Responsive, with or without feedback.
  3. Click Duplicate.
  4. Give your copy a name.
  5. In tab Templates, select your skin.
  6. Adapt the other tabs where necessary.
  7. Click OK.
